About Us
Cleaning Equipments

We provide all types of cleaning equipment for the paper and pulp industry, ensuring efficient contaminant removal and improved pulp quality.

Reject Sortor

We offer reliable reject sorters for the paper and pulp industry, ensuring effective separation of impurities and maximizing fiber recovery for enhanced productivity.

TDR Disc Refiner

TDR Disc Refiners for the paper and pulp industry, designed for efficient refining and fiber development. we ensure improved pulp quality, energy efficiency, and enhanced paper strength.

Pulpers

Our pulpers ensure efficient fiber slushing, contaminant removal, and energy-saving performance for optimal pulp processing.

Turbo

Turbo separators are crucial for efficiently removing heavy contaminants and improving pulp quality. We provide strong deflaking action and effective coarse screening, ensuring cleaner stock for further processing.

Pumps

Pumps are essential for transferring pulp, water, and chemicals throughout the production process. we ensure consistent flow, reduce maintenance, and improve overall operational performance.
