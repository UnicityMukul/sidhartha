De Trasher

  1. SIDDHARTHA INDUSTRIES' de-trasher is designed to extract reject stock from the pulper trashwell, aiding in the screening process and separating fibers from the pulp.
  2. The de-trasher is used for separating light impurities in a continuous pulping system.
  3. It helps maximize pulp recovery from waste paper.
  4. The de-trasher is also compatible with the Hicon pulper.
